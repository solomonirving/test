About the Name

My grandfather retired from the Los Angeles Fire Department in 1978. During his career, he worked for seven different stations around Los Angeles, CA. This is where “Seventh Ladder” comes into play. My grandfather was a huge role model in my life. My family and I unfortunately lost him in 2002 due to cancer. It is in me to give continued support to the fire departments, whether it’s a high five or hosting a cookout for them. My mission is to provide resources and funding to fire departments for education, training, emergency food, supplies, and repairs especially, during fire season.
